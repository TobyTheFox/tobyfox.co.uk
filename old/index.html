About me

Who am I? What do I do?

My name is Tobias Enefey Fox, and I'm a student currently doing his A levels. Here is a little bit of information about myself. Please note: clicking highlighted parts of the text will show relavant images. They can all be seen below too.


I was born in Sheffield, whereI still live, and at a young age I took an interest in computers & technology, as well as music. At around 7 years old I was bought a guitar, and I have been learning since.


In 2011 I learned HTML and started building upon my website. This is also about when I got my first Gibson guitar. In 2013 I went to Sicily hours before the Mt. Etna erruptions which I feel somewhat proud about. This is also the year I asked Lauren to prom.


On the day of prom (coincidently my 16th birthday) I was offered work maintaining and designing an ecommerce website for PJS Music in Dodworth, Barnlsley, and also working for a sound engineering company.


I got my GCSE results (a mixure of A*s and As, and a B) and headed off to Barnsley, where my favourate lesson was of course Computing. When it came to university choices I picked the University of York for its stunning Comp. Sci. course, for which I accepted their offere of a place. Then came the A level exams. TBC.
